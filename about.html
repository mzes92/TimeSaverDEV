About Us

   We are here to help you get on the waiting list of another public hospital's dental waiting list.We understand that you must be waiting for months or even years to get an appointment at that dentist. We are just a bunch of postgraduate students who would be glad if we could make your lives a little stress free and get you that dental appointment at a public dental hospitals as soon as we can. We strive to get you the most update waiting lists so that you can make an informed decision before switching to another hospitals waiting list if you are an existing patient. We also support new patients by giving them an option to look at all the public dental hospitals that are near them to get an appointment for general assessment.
